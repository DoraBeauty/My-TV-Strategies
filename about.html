About Elena

Passionate Lactation Consultant & Postpartum Recovery Specialist

Experience & Certification

With over 10 years of experience as an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC), I have helped thousands of mothers navigate the joys and challenges of breastfeeding.

My journey began in neonatal intensive care, where I witnessed the profound impact of personalized care. I believe every mother deserves evidence-based support tailored to her unique situation.

In addition to lactation, I specialize in postpartum body recovery. Understanding the physical changes a mother undergoes, I curated a line of medical-grade sculpting garments to support healing and confidence.

  • IBCLC Certified since 2012
  • Certified Postpartum Doula
  • Member of the International Lactation Consultant Association
